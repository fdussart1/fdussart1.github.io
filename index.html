Biography

Holder of a French High School Degree option Engineer science and European class, I naturally oriented myself towards a technical training by integrating the IUT of Nantes in order to carry out a Diploma University and Technological specialized in Mechanical Engineering and Productivity . Always attracted by beautiful objects and "DIY", I wanted to reconcile these areas in my training by choosing the University of Technology of Belfort-Montbéliard offering the engineering degree Ergonomics Design and Mechanical Engineering. During these three years of higher education I spent six months in Shanghai as part of a university exchange. Aviation, cars, design, drawing, music and meetings are part of my daily life. My dynamism, my commitment and my ambition lead me to give the best of myself in my work.
